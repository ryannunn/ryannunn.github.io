Improv

Ryan has performed with improv and sketch comedy groups in NYC, Chicago, and LA. At Northwestern, he was a member of the illustrious Mee-Ow sketch comedy group, as well as the long-form improv team Captain Lava. He has studied at both UCB and Groundlings.
