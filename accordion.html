My name is Christy Nunn, but my friends call me Monstah...

A nickname given to me by my father when one night as a small child I snuck outside and he found me hunting in the woods for unsuspecting animals. That nickname, like velcro on a shag carpet, stuck, and years later not much has changed. Except now I hunt in broad daylight. For marketing opportunities.
